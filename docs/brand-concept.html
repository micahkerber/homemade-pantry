Brand Tone Pillars
  • Warm & inviting — never stiff or corporate
  • Artisan & knowledgeable — she's the expert you trust
  • Unhurried & intentional — slow living, not slow service
  • Intimate — guests feel chosen, not just booked
  • Story-driven — every recipe, every jar has a reason
  • Texas-rooted — local, grounded, genuine
Elevator Pitch (30 sec)

The Homemade Pantry is an intimate, in-home culinary experience for small groups who want to slow down, connect, and learn the art of traditional kitchen crafts — sourdough, hand-churned butter, small-batch seasonal jam, and artisan baking & pastry — in a beautifully styled, unhurried setting. Hosted by Jaime Kerber and Brittany Trevett. You don't just watch. You make. And you leave with something real.

Words We Use / Words We Avoid
  • USE: gather, craft, slow, artisan, intentional, curated, warm, seasonal
  • AVOID: class, workshop, event, tutorial, beginner, basic, DIY
  • USE: experience, evening, gathering, made by hand, from scratch
  • AVOID: cheap, deal, sale, sign up, slot, available

Brand Identity · Experience Design

The Four Brand Pillars

Everything we create, host, and communicate should map back to these four truths.

🍞
Made by Hand
Every element is handcrafted — the bread, the butter, the jam, the pastry, the experience itself. Never rushed. Never packaged.
🕯️
Intentionally Slow
This is a counter-cultural offering. In a world of speed, we are an invitation to pause, breathe, and be fully present.
🫙
Gathered & Shared
The table is the point. Connection is the product. Everything guests make, they take — and the memory stays longer than the loaf.
🌿
Rooted & Real
Texas-based, home-hosted, and deeply personal. This isn't a brand — it's a person who genuinely loves what she does.
