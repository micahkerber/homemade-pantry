Logo Concepts

"Made slowly. Shared warmly."

Option A

The Gathered Table

A candle on a round table, viewed from above — three seats, a jar, a bowl. The table is always the point. Works for any experience.

Option B

The Offering

Two open hands holding a warm flame. "Made by hand, offered with warmth" — universal across sourdough, pastry, or anything they add.

Option C

Botanical Wreath

Wheat, herbs, and leaves in a circle — no single food dominant. Timeless artisan feel that works across every offering, every season.
