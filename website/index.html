The Experience

This is not
a baking class.
It's an evening.

The Homemade Pantry is an intimate, in-home culinary gathering designed for small groups who want to slow down, connect, and learn traditional kitchen skills in a warm, beautifully styled setting.

Each ~3 hour experience blends education, hands-on baking, and elevated grazing. You won't just watch — you'll shape, craft, stir, and taste. And you'll leave with everything you made.

And the evening can happen wherever you'd like it to — at our home kitchen in Brookshire, or in yours, if your space allows it. The choice is yours when you book.

Meet Your Hosts

A kitchen built
for gathering.

J
Jaime Kerber

Jaime Kerber · Founder

Jaime started the business after spending nearly twenty years in a demanding corporate career in Human Resources, where long hours, constant pressure, and always being "on" had become the norm. While the work was rewarding in many ways, it often pulled her away from the moments that mattered most — time with her children, slow mornings at home, and the kind of life that felt grounded and meaningful.

Over time, she realized she wanted more than a packed calendar and endless meetings. She wanted to build something with her own hands, create a life with more balance, and be present for her family in a way her corporate career never fully allowed. Slow mornings in the kitchen — hands in dough, cream turning to butter, the smell of something baking in the oven — became the part of the day that felt most real and fulfilling.

Baking wasn't just a hobby; it became a way to slow down, reconnect, and create comfort and joy for the people she loved. That feeling is what inspired the business. What began as a personal reset evolved into something rooted in family, warmth, and the belief that life's best moments are often the simplest ones.

B
Brittany Trevett

Brittany Trevett · Executive Baker

Brittany started the business as a way to reconnect with the passion that had always been at the center of her life — creating meaningful, beautiful experiences through baking. After stepping away from her culinary career to focus on raising her family, she realized how much she missed the creativity, connection, and joy that came from working in the kitchen and sharing that experience with others.

A culinary school graduate with more than 10 years of experience in professional bakeries, Brittany built a reputation for turning her clients' sweetest dreams into reality — from beautifully customized mini cookies to extravagant four-tier wedding cakes. If you could envision it, Brittany could create it.

But for Brittany, baking has always meant more than making desserts. It's about bringing people together, celebrating life's moments both big and small, and creating something made with care and love. She believes food made with love and your own hands doesn't just nourish the body — it feeds the heart and soul, too.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need any baking experience? ▾
Not at all. The Homemade Pantry is designed for complete beginners and experienced bakers alike. We guide you through every step — the goal is to make it feel natural and achievable, not intimidating.
Where is The Homemade Pantry located? ▾
You have two options when you book — both come with the same hands-on evening, the same take-home goods, and the same warmth.

Option 1 — Gather with us. Join us at our private home kitchen in Brookshire, TX — about 35 miles west of Houston. The exact address is shared upon confirmed booking.

Option 2 — We come to you. If you have the kitchen space, we'll host the evening in your home. Within a 15 mile radius of Brookshire is included; travel fees apply beyond that.

Just choose your preference on the booking form.
How long does the experience last? ▾
Plan for about 3 hours. Most guests find themselves lingering a little longer than expected — which is exactly the point.
Can I bring wine or drinks? ▾
Absolutely. You're welcome to bring your own wine or beverages. We provide water, a welcome drink, and the full charcuterie spread. It's your evening — make it yours.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? ▾
Please mention any dietary needs when booking and we'll do our best to accommodate. Note that the experience does involve gluten (sourdough) and dairy (butter). Reach out directly to discuss specific needs.
Can I purchase a gift card? ▾
Yes! Gift cards are available for all experience packages. They make a beautiful gift for birthdays, anniversaries, Mother's Day, or anyone who deserves an evening that's more than dinner out. Enquire via the booking form or email directly.
What is the cancellation policy? ▾
A 50% deposit is required at time of booking. Cancellations made more than 7 days before your experience receive a full deposit refund. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable but may be rescheduled subject to availability.
