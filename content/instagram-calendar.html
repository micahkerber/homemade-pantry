Strategy

6 Rules for The Pantry Feed

These will make or break the first 30 days.

01
Post 4–5x Per Week Max
Consistency beats volume. 4 great posts > 7 average ones. Rest days are strategic — the algorithm rewards saves and shares, not frequency.
02
Reels First, Always
Instagram currently pushes Reels to non-followers. At least 2 Reels per week gives The Homemade Pantry the best chance of discovery. Keep them under 30 seconds.
03
Reply to Every Comment
In the first 30 minutes after posting, reply to every comment. This signals to Instagram that the post is sparking conversation and boosts distribution significantly.
04
Stories Every Day
Even on non-post days, post 2–3 Stories. Behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&As, or a quick "good morning from the kitchen" keeps the account warm and the audience engaged.
05
Captions Tell the Story
Don't caption a photo of bread with "fresh loaf 🍞". Tell us the story — why this loaf, this morning, this moment. Emotion drives saves. Saves drive reach.
06
Local Hashtags Matter
Mix broad (#sourdough) with local (#HoustonFoodie, #BrooksshireTexas, #KatyTXFood). Local hashtags bring the right people — those who can actually attend.
